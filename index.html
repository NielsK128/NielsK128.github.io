About Me

Im a college student from Germany who started learning about game development back in 2018. I worked on and got to know most ends of the game development process as well as the publishing and profit-making. This includes working with programming languages like Java & C#, the UnityEngine and other software such as Blender. Lots of that was done in groups with other people. Many of the games I got my hands on have been released for various devices.
